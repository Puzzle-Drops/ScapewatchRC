How It Works

Scapewatch is an idle skilling game where your character automatically completes tasks, gains experience, and progresses through a vast world of activities. Set your goals, customize your skill preferences, and watch your character grow!

Tasks System

Your character automatically works through a queue of tasks. Each task involves gathering or processing items at specific nodes. Complete tasks to earn Rune Credits and unlock customization options.

Nodes & Activities

Travel between nodes across the world map. Each node offers different activities like mining ores, fishing, woodcutting, and more. Your character intelligently navigates and banks items when needed.

24 Skills

Master 24 unique skills including the exclusive Sailing skill! Each skill levels from 1 to 99, unlocking new activities and better rewards. Customize skill weights to focus on your favorites.

Pet Collection

Earn skill pets by completing tasks! Each skill has a unique pet with regular and shiny variants. Collect them all to show off your dedication and unlock the prestigious Max Cape.

Clue Scrolls

Discover clue scrolls while skilling! Complete multi-step treasure hunts across the world to earn caskets filled with valuable rewards. Five tiers from Easy to Master await brave adventurers.

Dynamic Shop

The shop rotates stock with each task completion. Buy supplies, resources, and runes at fluctuating prices. Smart shopping can accelerate your progress significantly!
